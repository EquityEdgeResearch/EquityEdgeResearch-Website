Leadership

Wyatt Leroux is an ambitious undergraduate student at the University of Waterloo, where he is pursuing a Bachelor of Accounting and Financial Management. With a profound enthusiasm for professional accounting and financial markets, Wyatt is deeply committed to driving innovation, fostering creativity, and promoting collaborative teamwork. Known for his exceptional attention to detail, strong organizational skills, and natural leadership ability, Wyatt aspires to leverage his expertise in capital markets and mergers & acquisitions as he progresses towards becoming a CPA and CFA

Wyatt's passion for finance ignited during his grade 11 co-op internship at Prosperity Solutions, a tax-centric advisory firm known for its innovative investment strategies. Here, Wyatt played a pivotal role in overseeing client acquisition and relationship management, handling initial consultations, and conducting RazorPlan assessments. His strategic integration of RazorPlan software led to a remarkable 400% increase in qualified prospects, demonstrating his keen eye for identifying client financial needs. Wyatt's knack for research became evident as he suggested new financial instruments for client portfolios, resulting in an additional 3% year-over-year return. It was this experience that sparked Wyatt's love for research and laid the foundation for his journey into equity research.

In parallel, Wyatt's commitment to community engagement was exemplified through his role as a Founding Advisory Council Member at Halton Regional Police Service's Youth Advisory Council. Leading a team of 8 individuals, Wyatt spearheaded the development of strategic marketing campaigns aimed at fostering positive relationships between youth and law enforcement. His initiatives, such as #myHRPS and #NoHateInHalton, were born out of meticulous survey analysis and aimed at bridging the gap between the community and the police force. Wyatt's dedication to conducting market research for potential policy initiatives further underscored his commitment to making a meaningful impact in his community.
